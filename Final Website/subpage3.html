Civil Discussion

The second tool to use to mitigate extremism is to encourage people to civilly discuss their views and beliefs. It is better to invite others to accept a new view or belief if we welcome them to accept it. To many times we villainize those that have differing views, but this does convince them that their views are wrong, it makes them believe that they are under attack and that anything to do with your belief is their enemy. If we can sit down and talk with peaceful and loving intentions, we will be able to avoid more extremism. Then, once everything has settled down, we can come together and find a solution to our problems or settle the conflict between our beliefs so that we can stand together on common ground.
